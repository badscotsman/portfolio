Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Scott, an expert programmer, published author, and obsessed game developer with 12 years of experience building games, mobile apps, and immersive XR experiences with Unity. With a background spanning 25+ years of experience in business software development, IT, and design, I've created solutions across various industries, including entertainment, education, architecture, and finance.

I love solving tough problems with clean code (usually with a bit of coffee), pushing creative boundaries (always having fun), and collaborating with passionate teams that care about doing great work to bring ideas to life – whether it's an indie game, a VR prototype, or something totally unexpected.

If you're looking for someone who knows their way around Unity and loves what they do, let's connect and build something awesome together!
